Resolve disputes with civility!

Process:

  • Bot replies to user mentions to initiate a 'Rock, Paper, Scissors' match.
  • Parties to the match then private message the bot as prompted.
  • After 5 minutes (or the above) the bot replies with the result.
  • Now deployed to Heroku!

About Me

Some people dream of working outdoors: sunlight, fresh air, exciting work — I’ve done that. It’s sweaty/chilling/wet, uncomfortable, fatiguing, and often incurs sunburn, insect bites, cartilage loss, etc.. And still, all paths eventually drift away from fieldwork: advancement progresses to leadership and that work is often done from above the fray.

So this last year I have been taking two college classes while working full-time (until recently). Occasionally taking an additional CodePath course or working on doing an extracurricular project. I am driven by goals: my patent, for another example, has been a motivating force for me since college. Lacking the funds for the patent has been instrumental in my moving to the Bay Area and building new connections.

I have accumulated many skills, but what has remained constant is my interest in solving problems. Whether it’s finding the most efficient way to build a deck, troubleshooting a small motor/electronic device, or debugging code — it’s problem solving that I like best.
